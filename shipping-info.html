Shipping

** COVID-19 CORONAVIRUS OUTBREAK UPDATE ** All our warehouses are currently operating but we are experiencing delivery delays. We will offer customer support by Chat and Email only.

All orders are being processsed but some orders are being delayed during delivery. UPS and USPS have put some shipment restrictions as well as limits to the number of packages they will pick up daily. Due to the shipment limits set by our carriers please expect some delays to your December orders. This is affecting all large suppliers and will probably continue through the holidays.

We apologize for these delays. Our customers have got used to getting their packages delivered on time and this is very difficult for our customer support team to have your orders delivered late. We are closely working with UPS and USPS and looking at alternate options to deliver your orders on time.

** There are some delays with some of our parcel carriers, transit times and shipment dates may be subject to some delays. **

** Due to high volumes and carrier capacity for this month only Orders under 15 pounds are shipping usps Please add 1-3 days to delivery day estimates **

Orders over 15 pounds are shipping ups and delivered within a few days from the estimated delivery date listed in your cart.

if there are any changes we will update the status here

Shipping is FREE for orders over $199. All other orders less than $199 ship for $9.99.
